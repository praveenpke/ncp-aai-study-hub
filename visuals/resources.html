Section 3 · Get your hands dirty

Frameworks to practice with

The exam is vendor-flavored but framework-literate: questions assume you recognize graph orchestration, multi-agent conversation, crew/role patterns, and the two open protocols. An afternoon with each pays off.

🕸️LangGraph

Graph-based agent orchestration with explicit state, checkpoints, and human-in-the-loop interrupts — the framework NVIDIA's own courses and toolkit examples use most.

💬AutoGen / AG2

Multi-agent conversation patterns (group chat, nested agents). AG2 is the community fork of the original AutoGen — know both names; "conversable agents" terminology shows up in questions.

👥CrewAI

Role/task/crew abstraction for quick multi-agent prototypes — the cleanest illustration of role-based delegation, a recurring architecture-question pattern.

🔌Model Context Protocol (MCP)

The open standard for agent ↔ tools/data connections. Know the client/server/host roles and the prompt-injection risks of untrusted servers — both are exam fodder.

🤝Agent2Agent Protocol (A2A)

Linux Foundation standard for agent ↔ agent communication (Agent Cards, JSON-RPC, SSE). The one-liner to memorize: MCP connects agents to tools; A2A connects agents to agents.

Section 5 · Pressure-test yourself

Practice & mock exams

Honest caveat: NVIDIA publishes no official practice exam for NCP-AAI. Everything below is community-built from the public blueprint — quality varies, question wording will not match the real exam, and none of these are endorsed by NVIDIA. Use them to find weak domains, not to memorize answers (and avoid anything marketed as "real exam dumps").

⚡FlashGenius — NCP-AAI Practice

500+ questions organized by the ten blueprint domains, with a free 10-questions/day tier — good for domain-by-domain drilling.

🧪Whizlabs — NCP-AAI Course

Scenario-based practice tests from an established cert-prep vendor; their free NCP-AAI guide is a decent blueprint summary.

🛠️Preporato — Practice Tests + Labs

Practice tests paired with hands-on labs — the lab angle is the differentiator, since the real exam rewards having actually built things.

🎓Udemy mock bundles

Several full-length mock sets exist, e.g. 6 Practice Exams (364 Qs) and Practice Exams: Agentic AI — cheap on sale; check recent reviews before buying, as quality is uneven.
